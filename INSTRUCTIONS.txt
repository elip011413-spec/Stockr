====================================
STOCKR — SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
====================================

STEP 1: Go to github.com and log in

STEP 2: Open your Stocks-finder repository

STEP 3: Tap "Add file" → "Upload files"

STEP 4: Upload the INDEX.HTML file from this zip
        (This is the only file GitHub needs!)

STEP 5: Tap "Commit changes"

STEP 6: Tap "Settings" at the top of your repo

STEP 7: Tap "Pages" on the left

STEP 8: Under Branch, select "main" then tap Save

STEP 9: Wait 60 seconds and refresh

STEP 10: Your live app link will appear in a green box!
         It will look like: https://yourusername.github.io/Stocks-finder

====================================
THAT'S IT! Bookmark the link on your phone.
